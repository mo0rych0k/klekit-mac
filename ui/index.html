KleKit

v0.1.0

KleKit is a lightweight, open-source voice-to-text utility that lets you dictate text directly into any input field. All processing happens entirely offline and on-device — meaning no internet connection is required, no cloud services are used, and no data ever leaves your hardware. Simply press a global hotkey, speak, and watch your spoken words get transcribed, refined by a local LLM, and pasted instantly at your active cursor.
